What we do with your money

When you deposit money into an ESB FX account, it’s protected in anumber of ways.

The essential

- Your money and assets (shares, for example) are never merged with ESB FX’s own money or assets
- ESB FX's global financial license is in the process of being licensed by London Finance Commission is an independent International Arbitrator in the Forex market
- ESB FX doesn’t use your money for business activities, including for hedging trades with other counterparties (or as margin for our own hedging trades)
